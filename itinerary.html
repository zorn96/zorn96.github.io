Weekend Attire Guide

Welcome Party

We ask that you dress in cocktail attire for the night before. Please avoid pastels or light grey that could come off as white or cream in photos, or a pattern with a white background.

Wedding Day

We ask that our guests please dress in formal evening wear, meaning floor length gowns and luxe fabrics for the femmes, and for our masc guests please dress in a stylish suit or tux. Add some personality and accessorize!

No special colors requested, we'd love to see everyone in a fun pop of color. However, for dresses, please avoid pastels or light grey that could come off as white or cream in photos, or a pattern with a white background. We also ask that you please generally avoid neons. Those who know, the bride can't with neons.

We'd love to see you looking and feeling your best, can never be too formal!

Sendoff Brunch

Feel free to dress casual, the bride and groom will be happy to give you a hug regardless.
